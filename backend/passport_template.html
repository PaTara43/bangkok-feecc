This is a web3 passport of your picture. It proofs the origin and provides data harvested during painting session.   1. Connect to the Wi-FI point named RobonomicsSensor-*
  2. Proceed to 192.168.4.1
  3. Enter your Wi-Fi credentials 
  4. The sensor will reboot and continue publishing data to the Robonomics Polkadot Parachain
